About

I’m a Postdoctoral Associate in the Division of Applied Math, Brown University, working with Prof. Stuart Geman. Previously, I got my Ph.D. in Applied Math at Brown University, and my bachelor degrees from Peking University. Here’s my CV.

I’m interested in using probabilistic and statistical methods to understand

  • Kinetics v.s. Thermodynamics in the folding of RNA molecules
  • Compositional Probabilistic Generative Models for Computer Vision

My methodological research has focused on

  • Theory and algorithm to overcome entropic barriers in stochastic dynamics
  • Multiscale optimization for efficient training of deep neural networks
  • Gradient-based MCMC methods for discrete random variables
